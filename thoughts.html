O1: Modeling Natural Language Chain of Thought as a Reinforcement Learning Problem

Last week, OpenAI released their newest model, O1. Following their recent trend of witholding information about their research, they released a white paper that makes a few ambiguous references to what they seem to be doing. They do, however, reveal in depth O1's impressive benchmark results.

Incorporating planning based methods (RL in particular) with LLM training has been an exciting problem a number of researchers have been working towards. It's the kind of solution that seems intuitive and obvious to people on the outside, but is actually incredibly hard to pull off (for reasons I'll go into further).

I thought it would be a good exercise for me to review surrounding literature to try and interpret what OpenAI might be doing here, and that's what this "article" is going to be about. Before getting into it, I wanted to be upfront about what this will include and what it will not include.
